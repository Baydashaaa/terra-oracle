How it works

Terra Oracle

Anonymous Q&A on Terra Classic.
Ask anything. Answer freely. Earn reputation.

Step by step
🔗
Connect your wallet
Your identity stays anonymous - wallet address is never shown publicly.
❓
Ask a question - 200,000 LUNC
Pay once to submit. Fee is split: 50% goes to the Weekly Draw prize pool, 50% to Protocol Treasury. Asking also gives you +2 free Draw entries.
💬
Anyone can answer - for free
No payment needed to answer. Community upvotes the best answers. Top answerers build reputation over time.
💰
DAO Chat - 5,000 LUNC per message
On-chain messages stored forever on Terra Classic. Every 10 messages per day earns +1 free lottery entry (max 2/day).
🎰
Oracle Draw - win the prize pool
Prize pool draws happen daily at 20:00 UTC and weekly every Monday at 20:00 UTC. Earn free entries for the Weekly Draw through Q&A and Chat activity.
