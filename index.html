Protocol Info

Terra Oracle

Anonymous intelligence layer for Terra Classic.
Built for those who know — but can't always say.

Why it exists

Some truths can only be spoken anonymously

Validators, developers, and long-term community members carry privileged knowledge about Terra Classic — governance dynamics, technical risks, whale behaviour, internal conflicts. But public identity creates a cost for honesty: delegation risk, community backlash, competitive exposure.

Terra Oracle is a protocol where that knowledge can surface — with real skin in the game, without the reputational risk.

Who it's for

If you've ever held back because of who might be watching

Validator · before a governance vote
"I think this proposal is harmful to the network, but voting No publicly means conflict with the team behind it."
Developer · after reviewing a PR
"There's a real issue here but if I raise it publicly it'll look like I'm attacking a contributor I have history with."
Longtime holder · watching the market
"I know what the actual supply situation looks like — but I'm not a public voice and nobody would listen anyway."
Before governance votes

Terra Oracle as a pre-vote signal layer

When a new proposal drops, Oracle becomes the place to gauge real validator sentiment — before anyone has committed publicly. Ask what validators actually think. See what concerns aren't making it into the official discussion. Get signal without the theatre.

The goal is not to replace governance. It's to make the conversation before governance more honest.

Pay 200,000 LUNC to ask. Anyone can answer for free. Community votes answers up or down. Best answers earn titles — and a share of the reward pool.
