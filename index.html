Terra Oracle Classic

An on-chain Q&A and governance system where every action has economic weight.
No login. Only wallet.

All transactions verifiable on Terra Classic blockchain

What is a question?

A question on Terra Oracle is not just text — it's an economic action on the blockchain. When you pay 200,000 LUNC to ask, that fee is split automatically:

50%
→ Weekly Prize Pool
50%
→ Protocol Treasury

You also receive +40 REP and +2 free Draw entries — your contribution immediately returns value back to you. Oracle Draw entries also earn +10 REP per entry.

Anonymity · Web3 native
🕶
No login. No email. No account.

Your identity is your wallet address — and it's never shown publicly. The system uses an anonymous alias generated from your address. You participate fully without revealing who you are. This is a core Web3 principle: sovereignty over your own identity.

Reputation = real value

REP is not just points. It's your position in the protocol — and it directly affects how much LUNC you earn from the weekly pool.

Fee discount
Higher rank = lower cost per question. Up to −25% at Ascended rank.
Reward multiplier
Your weekly pool share is multiplied by your rank. Up to ×3.0 at Ascended.
